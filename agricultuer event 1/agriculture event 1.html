Program Activities

Organic Farming Training

Comprehensive training on organic farming techniques, natural fertilizers, and sustainable practices

Chemical-Free Methods

Teaching farmers to reduce chemical dependency and adopt eco-friendly pest control

Sapling Distribution

Distribution of saplings to farmers for promoting afforestation and sustainable agriculture

Jeevamrut

Training on preparing and using Jeevamrut, a natural microbial culture for enriching soil fertility

Beejamrut

Demonstration of Beejamrut preparation for organic seed treatment and improved germination

Community Support

Building farmer networks for knowledge sharing and collective organic farming initiatives
