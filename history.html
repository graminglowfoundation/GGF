Milestones of Impact

Every milestone represents lives transformed, communities empowered, and sustainable change implemented across rural India with dedication and innovation

April 2025

Foundation Established

Gramin Glow Foundation,Egra was officially incorporated as a registered non-profit organization with a clear mission to transform rural communities through comprehensive development programs, educational initiatives, and sustainable social welfare services.

July 11, 2025

Health Awareness Initiative

Successfully launched "Swasthya Bornomala" - a comprehensive Health & Hygiene Awareness Campaign in strategic partnership with Mallikpur Primary School, directly impacting over 200 community members with essential health education, free medical screenings, and preventive care guidance.

View Campaign Details
August 16, 2025

Digital Transformation

Launched our comprehensive digital platform to enhance organizational transparency, facilitate seamless donor engagement, and provide real-time updates on community impact initiatives. This digital milestone reinforces our commitment to accountable NGO practices with global connectivity.

4th November, 2025

Humanity in Action

Grameen Glow Foundation,Egra reached out to help a mentally challenged and homeless man who was injured in a minor road accident near Tajpur Bus Stand. The team provided first aid, food, and comfort, and informed local authorities to ensure his continued care — reaffirming the foundation’s commitment to humanity and compassion. ❤️
