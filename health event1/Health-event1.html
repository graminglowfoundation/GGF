Live demonstrations on proper handwashing techniques, toothbrushing methods, and personal hygiene practices

Engaging quiz sessions with small prizes to reinforce learning and make the experience memorable for children

Teaching children about germs, how they spread, and the importance of maintaining cleanliness to prevent diseases
